This application will be a data base for solar sales to store information about prospective sales as well as completed sales

MVP

Users will have their own profile to store information
Two seperate tables for sets/bids and completed sales
Ability to add and delete rows/clients from tables
Ability to sort tables by rows and columns
Ability to add notes to the table to reference what was talked about with clients
Monthly bill calculator


Future features

Search bar for users to look for specific customers
Ways for rep to add external documentation to database (permits, docs, etc...)
